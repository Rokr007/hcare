Smart HealthCare

Introduction

Smart HealthCare System is an integrated environment whose main aim is to bring all modules together into a single system comprising of blood donation, doctor's advice, health tips and directories of hospitals and ambulances.

It specializes in helping organisations develop, deploy and support online health care initiatives to achieve the very best patient outcomes.

Apart from this,you can ask your queries from our qualified medical Doctors and proper instructions to keep oneself disease free.

The Portal provides a platform that provides effective quality HealthCare Services with less IT set-up costs and reduced risk.

It consists of 4 modules:

  • Blood Bank Portal
  • Doctors Consultation Forum
  • Directory Services
  • Healthy Living
